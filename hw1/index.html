  • Favorite part: I loved the AirBnb example and how the author paid attention to the filler words in the search bar. Those default words (e.g. "John Doe" when entering a first and last name for an app) are often times very boring and used to show users HOW to use a component within an app. AirBnb, however, takes a different approach and uses those default words to show WHAT'S POSSIBLE when using AirBnb to instill a sense of wonder in the users.
