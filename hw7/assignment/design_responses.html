  • What is something you learned from this article? (3-4 sentences)

    In this article, I learned about the Gestalt principles, developed by German psychologists in 1920s. More concretely, I learned:

      Emergence relates to proximity, common regions, and similarity, that is elements arranged close to each other, or in a similar region, or similar in shape or form, respectively, are perceived as more related than those placed further apart.
      Reification and Invariance relates to closure, that is where a group of elements are often perceived to be a single recognisable form or figure. This is often the case for the use of symbols in UI design.
      Multi-Stability relates to the use of symmetry, as this comfort helps us make sense of the world and interpret it in ways that make sense to us.
